Clinical summary: The summary sensitivity and specificity are in the forest plot below. The reported accuracy is not sufficient to confirm or exclude outcomes at the prevalence of outcomes studied as the positive predictive value is 88% and the negative predictive value is 10%. However, the quality of evidence is low for all outcomes due to imprecision and indirectness of results. Regarding the detection of subsegmental emboli, the positive predictive value pooled from 6 studies is --% (embolism was confirmed in -- of -- patients)

We included prospective, cross-sectional diagnostic test assessment studies in which all patients received both index text (computed tomographic pulmonary angiography) and reference tests.

Methods overview:   Studies were abstacted into standardized tables of , and results. This repository updates a previously published meta-analysis.(1) Newer studies included are listed in the references. Rationale for newer trials excluded may be listed at the end of the references. See methods for details.
